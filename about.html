Patrick Eseme's Story

Patrick Eseme was born on June, 8th 1997 in Nakuru, Kenya. He was raised by his mother, Alice Kikwai and his Dad, Ronald Adorot together wis his four brothers and one sister. All through Eseme's life, he used humour as a tool to bring joy and happiness to those around him.

After graduating from highschool, he started a stand up comedy in clubs and shows organised by different parties with the aid of his long time friend and relative Elvis Mutoro.

He then moved to Nairobi to pursue his course in Aeronautical Engineering and later on launched a logistics company, Eseme Logistics and a talent hub, The Link Africa.

Patrick began pursuing a career in stand-up comedy after performing at the KOM awards.
